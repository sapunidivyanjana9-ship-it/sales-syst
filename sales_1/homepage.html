About Us

We Provide The Best Since 2020

Pearl Land Commodities (Pvt) Ltd, a company established for providing value added local healthy food options under the brand name "PELCOMO". Led by a team passionate about rural development, they are the exclusive provider of premium spices and dehydrated foods, renowned for quality and purity.

With expertise in traditional food processing, PELCOMO goes beyond business, offering sustainable livelihood opportunities for rural farmers through the "Kadanhena Sawbagya Community Development Project".

✨ We care our planet and future with the power of our passionate and competent human capital.
